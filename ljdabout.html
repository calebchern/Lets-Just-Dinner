Who are we?

We are a local start-up that aims to reduce meal prepping times so that you can have that extra time to push harder and go the extra mile; all while keeping cost to a minimum.

What are our aims?

We aim to reduce meal prepping times for all! By pre-portioning, cutting and washing the base ingredients, cooking will be a breeze! Even for the average Joe!

We strive to ensure that it will take no longer than 15 minutes to create our meals and each meal kit comes with simple step by step instructions that will be easy to follow!
